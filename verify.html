How Verification Works
1
The identity owner logs into their SecureID dashboard and clicks "Generate Share Code".
2
A unique 6-character code is created, valid for 30 minutes.
3
They share the code with the verifying party (you).
4
Enter the code above to instantly see the verified identity details.
⚠ In production: codes would be encrypted, single-use, and transmitted over TLS. OTP SMS would be supported.
